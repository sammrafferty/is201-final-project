Favorite Bike Rides

Here are my top three road rides in the western US, ranked. Each one has a few notes below it.

  1. LoToJa (Logan, UT to Jackson, WY)
    • 206 miles in a single day — the longest single-day road race in the country
    • Crosses three states: Utah, Idaho, and Wyoming
    • Finished it last year — one of the hardest things I've done
  2. Alpine Loop
    • About 20 miles, a loop through American Fork and Provo Canyons
    • Roughly 3,000 feet of climbing
    • Best in the fall when the leaves are changing
  3. Provo Canyon
    • Easy paved trail, great for a recovery day
    • Follows the river the whole way up
    • The go-to ride when I'm short on time
